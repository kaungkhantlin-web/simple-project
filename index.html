Disney Jr. is a brand of children's television channels owned and operated by The Walt Disney Company, aimed at preschoolers between 2 and 7 years old. Since its American debut on February 14, 2011, the brand was launched worldwide. In most countries the channel was formerly known as Playhouse Disney. Disney Junior started closing internationally in 2019, mostly in Europe and Asia-Pacific, as it transitioned viewers to Disney+ and Disney Channel's morning blocks, while other versions remain.
